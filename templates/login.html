Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about PostFlow.

Is it good for me?

You can schedule a post with text + image on Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, X.

✍️ Facebook public page and Instagram professional account need to be linked in Meta Business Suite. Posts will be published on the Facebook public page and the Instagram professional account.

  • 1 Facebook Public Page
  • 1 Instagram Professional account

✍️ You'll need a TikTok business account. Posts will be published on that TikTok business account.

  • 1 TikTok business account

✍️ You'll need a LinkedIn account. Posts will be published on that LinkedIn account.

  • 1 LinkedIn account

✍️ An X account is enough. Posts will be published on that X account.

  • 1 X account
How many posts can I send per day?

You can make 2 posts per day on each platform. Ex: X: 2 posts, Facebook: 2 posts, TikTok: 2 posts etc. Trust me — it's more than enough.

Can I post reels (videos)?

Yes, on Facebook, Instagram and TikTok. YouTube will be added soon. Videos will be processed to ensure they meet minimum requirements for publishing. Same with images — even if you post just text, it will be converted to an image (super useful for Instagram).

Does it have analytics?

No. Turn on notifications — if you have many notifications you did good, if not, you need to improve. PostFlow takes the practical approach. Check the social media apps directly for detailed data.

Why is posting consistently important?

It helps you get in front of your leads. It helps potential clients choose you over competition. It's about building trust and showing how you can help.

What if I don't like this app?

You have 7 days free to test it. No commitment, no credit card required upfront.

How will I pay if I like it?

Old school wire bank transfer.

Why don't I see Threads, Mastodon, Bluesky etc.?

They are not currently supported.

Why only login with Google?

It's a reliable way to verify real accounts. Everyone has a Google account nowadays.
